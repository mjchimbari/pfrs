Help & Instructions

Step-by-step guide for all users

Submitting Your Responses

  1. Sign in with your student number and the password from your feedback letter.
  2. All reviewer comments for your proposal are shown one by one.
  3. For each ★ Required comment:
    • The ✓ I have addressed this button is pre-selected — keep it if you made the change.
    • Click ↩ I wish to rebut this if you disagree with the comment.
    • Write your explanation in the response field.
    • Write the exact page and section reference in the location field.
  4. Your work is saved automatically as you type.
  5. Scroll down and upload your revised proposal (PDF, Word or ZIP · max 20 MB). This is required.
  6. Click Submit to Supervisor →
Tip: Comments marked "✓ For information only" do not need a response.

If Your Supervisor Returns Your Responses

  1. Log in — a red banner shows your supervisor's written feedback.
  2. Click Revise & Resubmit → — your previous responses are still there.
  3. Make changes, re-upload your proposal if needed, and submit again.
  4. This loop repeats until your supervisor approves.
Once approved you will see "🎉 Sent to Faculty". No further action needed.

Workflow Stages

  • Student Responds — you are filling in responses.
  • Supervisor Review — awaiting your supervisor.
  • Approved — supervisor is satisfied.
  • Faculty Received — sent to the Postgraduate Coordinator.

Reviewing Student Responses

  1. Sign in with your Supervisor ID (e.g. SUP001) and password.
  2. Students with ⏳ Awaiting Review need your attention.
  3. Click 👁 Review Responses on a student card.
  4. Read each comment alongside the student's response. Green = addressed; amber = rebuttal.
  5. Check that a revised proposal has been uploaded — download it with the 📥 button.
  6. If satisfied: click ✓ Approve & Send to Faculty. The coordinator and student are notified automatically.
  7. If revisions needed: click ↩ Return to Student, write specific feedback, click Send.
Tip: You can return responses as many times as needed. Every round is recorded in the audit log.

Chair Login

  1. Click 🔑 Chair / Coord on the login screen.
  2. Username: chair · Password: [as provided]
  3. Or use ⚡ Quick Chair Access for demo mode.

Coordinator Login

  1. Username: coord · Password: [as provided]
  2. You see only students whose responses have been approved by their supervisor.

Downloading the PDF Response Record

  1. Find the student in the Overview tab — approved students show a green 📥 PDF button.
  2. Or open a student's detail view — a prominent 📥 Download PDF banner appears at the top.
  3. Also available in the Notifications tab next to each coordinator notification.
  4. Clicking opens a print dialog — set Destination: Save as PDF, orientation: Landscape, click Save.
Tip: Set margins to Minimum in the print dialog for best results.

Managing Passwords

  • Go to the 🔑 Passwords tab.
  • Click Reset next to any student or supervisor.
  • New password takes effect immediately. Share securely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Submit button is not working.

All ★ required fields (response text + location) must be complete. Incomplete fields turn red. You must also upload your revised proposal.

I closed the browser — is my work lost?

No. Responses save automatically as you type and on every Save Draft. They are stored in your browser and will be there when you reopen on the same device.

What is the difference between Addressed and Rebuttal?

Addressed = you agree and made the change. Rebuttal = you disagree and argue, with evidence, why the comment does not apply. Both require a written explanation and location reference.

How do I save as PDF — I see a print dialog?

In the print dialog, click the Destination dropdown and select Save as PDF. Set orientation to Landscape. Click Save.

Can I see responses on a different device?

Yes — all responses and uploaded proposal files are stored in the cloud and are accessible from any device. Supervisors and co-supervisors can log in from their own computers and download the revised proposal.

I forgot my password.

Contact your supervisor or the Postgraduate Coordinator. The chair can reset passwords from the 🔑 Passwords tab.
